About event

Let’s get into the lesser discussed topics with some of New York’s top designers. This edition we dive into how we collaborate with engineers. What’s the secret sauce to doin this well?

We have our very own Ashwini who will bring a ton of experience with her from companies such as Facebook, Microsoft and Zocdoc. We have Barton who’s working on something very related to this subject at Spotify. We have Danny (who’s a really good coder himself actually) who used to run NYC’s Framer meetups and spoke at their Loupe conference in Amsterdam. Then we have Hannah who works at Google and used to work at UsTwo in Sydney, giving us the perspective of both agency and large tech co.

We invite designers of all levels to join us to meet, learn, and have a good time.
