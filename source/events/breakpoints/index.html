About event

At Breakpoints, we discuss the lesser known aspects of design careers — the things we do daily that are outside the latest tools and trends. In this edition, we’ll get into a core part of our job — collaboration with engineering. We’ll chat with a panel of some of New York’s top designers and hear about their experience and tips for success.

We have our very own Ashwini, who brings her past experiences from companies such as Facebook, Microsoft and Zocdoc. We have Barton who’s currently collaborating very closely with engineers on a new project at Spotify. Danny, who as both a designer and a coder, brings perspectives from both sides and can speak to his experience from running NYC Framer meetups and his work at Kickstarter. And Hannah, who is currently at Google, will bring her previous experiences from Foursquare and Ustwo — sharing a comparative perspective on collaboration in small agency settings VS big tech.

We invite designers of all levels to join us to meet, learn, and have a good time.
